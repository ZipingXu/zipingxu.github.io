I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Statistics at Harvard University with Professor [Susan Murphy](http://people.seas.harvard.edu/~samurphy/).

I obtained my PhD degree in Statistics from University of Michigan advised by [Ambuj Tewari](https://ambujtewari.github.io). Before joining University of Michigan, I obtained my B.S. degree in Data Science from Peking University in 2018, where I was advised by Professor [Song Xi Chen](https://www.songxichen.com).

**Research Interests.** Machine Learning theory, especially on Reinforcement Learning (RL), Transfer Learning and Multitask Learning. I am also interested in a wide range of scientific and healthcare applications of ML.
